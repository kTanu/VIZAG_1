Showing Top 10 Hotels in Visakhapatnam

The Park Hotel

Located on the lovely Beach Road, The Park Hotel is an ideal accommodation option for both business as well as leisure travellers. The property is in proximity to the beach, overlooking the great Bay of Bengal. It boasts of an outdoor swimming pool, a big tennis court and a well-equipped gymnasium for the convenience of guests. At the hotel’s spa, guests can pamper themselves with massages and other therapies. Accommodation at the hotel is offered in 66 elegant rooms that available in Executive Rooms and Suites categories. These guestrooms are provided with LCD TV, electronic safe, tea/coffee maker, mini bar and tea/coffee maker. For the convenience of business guests, the property also features a well-appointed business hall that is perfect for holding conferences and seminars. When it comes to dine and wine, the property offers some amazing options, including fine dining venue Chef’s Table, the poolside Aqua and the sophisticated Awesome Arc.

The Gateway Hotel

Another plush property in Visakhapatnam, The Gateway Hotel on Beach Road will impress you with its grand architecture and warm hospitality. Just a few minutes’ walk away from the beach, this property has all the ingredients to make your holiday a memorable experience. Visakhapatnam Railway Station and airport are easily accessible from here. Among several prime facilities offered at the hotel, guests really praise the hotel’s outdoor pool, spa centre and fully equipped business centre. Airport shuttle, doctor-on-call, currency exchange, car hire and laundry are some of the other services offered-on-request at the hotel. Flat-screen TV, mini bar and tea/coffee maker are offered in all the beautifully designed and classy guestrooms that are available in Standard, Executive Sea Views and Executive Suite room categories. The on-site dining venues GAD, Ming Garden and Lawson’s Lounge are perfect for a classy fine dine experience.

Hotel Grand Bay

WelcomHotel Grand Bay is a premium property, located on Beach Road, which offers unparalleled services to guests. While Indira Gandhi Zoological Park is about 7 km from the property, the famous Daspalla Hills is just 10 minutes’ drive away. An array of premium services and facilities are offered on site like a well-appointed gymnasium, an outdoor swimming pool, spa centre and a billiards room. Accommodation at the hotel is offered in 104 classy yet chic, spacious and fully furnished rooms that are categorised as Executive Club Exclusive, Deluxe Room, Superior Suite and Deluxe Suite. All these gorgeous and modern guestrooms are fitted with amenities, such as internet, LCD TV and tea/coffee maker. When it comes to food, the property has plenty options, including Dakshin, Welcome Oceanic, The Residency and the exclusive Marcopolo Lounge Bar. A variety of delicious delicacies and exotic cocktails can be relished at these exotic wine and dine venues

Dolphin Hotel Vishakapatnam

Set in a commercial district with cinemas, shops and restaurants, this informal hotel is 1 km from Visakhapatnam railway station, 3 km from Ramakrishna Beach and 4 km from the INS Kursura, a decommissioned submarine with tours. Down-to-earth rooms provide flat-screen TVs and minibars, plus tea and coffeemaking facilities. Upgraded rooms come with Wi-Fi access. Suites add living areas. Room service is offered 24/7. An airy restaurant features city views and regular live music. There's also a wood-panelled bar and a 24-hour coffee shop. Other amenities include an outdoor pool and a fitness centre. Parking is available.

Novotel Visakhapatnam Varun Beach

Spoil yourself in 5-star seafront luxury at Novotel Visakhapatnam Varun Beach hotel. Spare contemporary design makes your room a calm haven, and picture windows offer you dazzling Bay of Bengal views. Rise and shine with a dip in the infinity pool and breakfast with an ocean backdrop at the lavish Indian and Western buffet at Novotel. The hotel shares a close proximity with the city railway station, Rushikonda Beach, Vizag Port and major hospitals. The popular Submarine Museum is just a walk away. The hotel's city centre location makes it approachable from industrial and business hubs. Novotel Vizag forms part of a complex consisting of an INOX multiplex, night club and mall. Novotel Visakhapatnam Varun Beach is among the only hotels in Visakhapatnam near a beach. The hotel offers calming views of the sea from every nook and corner.

Four Points By Sheraton Visakhapatnam

Centrally located at Waltair Road, Four Points by Sheraton Visakhapatnam offers rooms with 32-inch flat-screen TVs and rain showers. It offers free parking, complimentary Wi-Fi to the guests and ozonated outdoor pool and 24-hour concierge service. RK beach and Submarine Museum are 1.2 mi while Vuda Park, WIPRO are 0.6 mi away. Andhra University is 1.9 mi, and Naval Dockyard is 11 mi from the property. Four Points by Sheraton Visakhapatnam is 5 mins drive from Ramakrishna Beach and 20 mins drive from Kailashgiri. It is 25 minutes drive from Visakhapatnam Airport. The hotel offers spacious rooms with comfort of premium bedding, an armchair and ample workspace. It comes with a tea & coffeemaker and free daily newspapers. The hotel houses a business center and tour desk. The Eatery offers local and international dining while Best Brews bar offers beverages.
